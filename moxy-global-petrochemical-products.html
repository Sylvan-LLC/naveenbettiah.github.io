Petrochemical Products

Aromatics

Moxy Global is a leading suppliers of the group of petrochemicals known as ‘aromatics’ which includes benzene, toluene and xylene. These colourless liquids with a characteristic smell are important chemical precursors or ‘building blocks’ which can be converted into many of the intermediates and polymers needed to produce an extensive range of everyday products.

Polymers

Polymers are used in the commercial production of plastics, elastomers, man-made fibres, adhesives, and surface coatings, a tremendous variety of polymers are used. There are many ways to classify these compounds.

See Product Constituents

Glycols

Glycols are liquid chemicals formulated from natural gas and crude oil. You will find glycols in many every day house hold items including engine coolants, plastic bottles and polyester fabric. In a glycol molecule, two hydroxyl (-OH) groups are attached to different carbon atoms. The most common and simple types of glycol are ethylene glycol and propylene glycol.

See Product Constituents

Amines

Amines are organic derivatives of ammonia, in which one, two, or all three of the hydrogens of ammonia are replaced by organic groups. Compounds RNH­2 are called primary amines, R2NH secondary amines, and R3N are tertiary amines.
